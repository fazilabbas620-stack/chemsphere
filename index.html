M.Phil — Inorganic Analytical Chemistry

A Chemist's Journey

Welcome to ChemSphere. I am a passionate chemistry educator currently pursuing my M.Phil in Inorganic Analytical Chemistry, dedicated to making complex chemical concepts accessible and engaging for every student.

My research focuses on the intersection of inorganic synthesis and analytical techniques — a field that bridges the molecular architecture of matter with the precision of measurement science. I believe that true understanding in chemistry comes not from memorization, but from developing intuition about why atoms and molecules behave the way they do.

"Every element on the periodic table tells a story. Every titration reveals a truth. My goal is to help you read those stories and discover those truths for yourself."
